Hi, What can we help you with?

Listed below are answers to our frequently asked questions. They are categorized and you can browse throgh by narrowing down on the category.Please go through them to get your questions answered the quickest.

What is the refund policy?
  • Cancellation 5 Days Prior To The Date Of Departure – 80% refund.
  • Cancellation 2 Days Prior To The Date Of Departure – 25% refund.
  • Cancellation 2 Days Prior with carry forward of the amount for future event – Free
  • In case we cancel any of the event, we will make 100% refund provided the booking is done on our website.
How do I make a reservation?
Reservations can be made year round by calling or emailing our Office. Advance booking is recommended and a confirmation deposit is required to secure your booking, the balance is due 10 days prior to trip departure, or at the time of booking if within 10 days. All prices include Service Tax and appropriate user and permit fees. We reserve the right to change these prices if there is an excessive increase in our costs.


How do I make a cancellation?
We reserve the right to cancel any trip due to adverse river, road or weather conditions, or insufficient bookings - in these circumstances trip payments are refunded in full. For more details please go through the refund policy
